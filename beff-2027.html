BEFF 2027

Brussels Experimental Film Festival

Date February 2027
Duration 4 days
Organizer Tawanen ASBL

About the Festival

BEFF is a new festival dedicated to experimental cinema and video art.

Programme

  • Film competitions — short and feature
  • Curated experimental and video art screenings
  • Video art exhibition — Cinema Gallery
  • Audiovisual performances
  • Daily music programme
  • Talks, Q&A, and panel discussions

Opening Programme

Premiere of the collective film from the Urban Temporalities lab.

Inclusive Film Lab

Workshops for deaf and hard-of-hearing participants.

Guest Homages

Honouring pioneering voices in experimental cinema:

Chantal Akerman

Hala Alabdalla

Farah Kassem

Michel Khleifi

Objectives

Support emerging filmmakers and strengthen Brussels' cultural landscape.

Target Audience

Artists, filmmakers, students, cultural professionals, and curious audiences.
